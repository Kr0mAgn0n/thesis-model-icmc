	a menos de isometrias da esfera $\Sp^3$, o toro de Clifford \'e
	a \'unica superf\'icie m\'inima, mergulhada e de genus $1$ em 
	$\Sp^3$. Neste trabalho apresentaremos a demonstra\c c\~ao
	da conjectura de Lawson obtida em 2013 por Simon Brende
	\cite{Brendle2013a}.
}{Superf\'icies m\'inimas, Conjectura de Lawson, Toro de Clifford}

\textoresumo[english]{
%	This work will develop the proof of Lawson's conjecture by Simon Brendle in \cite{Brendle2013a}.
%	The conjceture was published by H. Lawson in \cite{Lawson1970a}.
%	The proof use the maximum principle for two-pointed functions developed by Huisken in \cite{Huisken1998}.

	embedded minimal surface in $\Sp^3$ of genus $1$, up to rigid 
	motions in $\Sp^3$. In this work we present the proof of this
	conjecture obtained in 2013 by Simon Brende \cite{Brendle2013a}.
}{Minimal surfaces, Lawson conjecture, Clifford torus}
